¡Hola! I was born and raised in Barcelona, a ___-Mediterranean heaven with friendly faces and too much ️--sun.
With 15 years of experience creating award-winning digital products, I ventured into developing a visual language for Cocoon, Els Amics de les Arts and the United Nations associated DigitalArt4Climate for COP26.

In a bleeding edge of Web 3.0 I converted my passion for digital art into the driving force to build the project which is here to make history - Exquisite Workers.

I champion the most world's gifted illustrators opening doors to Metaverse to brands and global projects. Over 500 highly skilled visual & graphic artisans, we share a craving for decentralized digital economy, traveling to space and the Surrealist game "Exquisite Corpse".

Excited about discovering the subconscious universe, I created Hypnagogies, featured on Wetransfer, TimeOut and Visions Gallery in Barcelona as a solo show in June 2011 to make you dive into the hypnotic state.
